Welcome to Lakshmi Mess. Call us for Party Orders / Outdoor Catering at 72006 98989.

Lakshmi Mess is known for healthy and tasty food around Thuraiyur. We take care of our customers by providing Herbal water, Serving food on Clean Banana Leaf, Herbal tiffin like Kalan Chapathi, Vazhapoo Dosai, Vazha Thandu Soup, Kids favourite tiffin like Cheese Dosai and much more.

Just visit once to taste the homemade like food. We also undertake party orders for Marriage and other functions. We do catering for marriage and other functions, in and around Thuraiyur, Perambalur and Trichy. We do Dosa live counter with 3 herbal varieties.

  • We serve Veg Indian Food
  • Varieties of Herbal Dosai
  • We serve Herbal Water
  • Dosa live counter on any of your functions
